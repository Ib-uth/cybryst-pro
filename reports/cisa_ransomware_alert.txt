CYBERSECURITY & INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY AGENCY
ALERT (AA24-083A)
Ransomware Targeting Healthcare and Public Health Sector

Original release date: March 15, 2024
Last revised: March 20, 2024

SUMMARY
CISA, the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), and the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) are releasing this joint Cybersecurity Advisory (CSA) to provide information on Royal and BlackCat ransomware variants actively targeting Healthcare and Public Health (HPH) sector organizations.

TACTICS, TECHNIQUES, AND PROCEDURES (TTPs)

INITIAL ACCESS
Threat actors gain initial access through:
- Exploiting vulnerabilities in internet-facing applications
- Phishing campaigns targeting healthcare staff
- Compromising remote desktop protocol (RDP) connections
- Supply chain attacks targeting healthcare software vendors

RECOMMENDATIONS
Immediate Actions:
1. Implement network segmentation
2. Enable multi-factor authentication
3. Deploy endpoint detection and response (EDR) solutions
4. Conduct security awareness training
5. Implement backup and recovery procedures

Long-term Measures:
1. Establish incident response capabilities
2. Implement zero-trust architecture
3. Regular vulnerability assessments
4. Security monitoring and logging
5. Cyber threat intelligence integration
